About

Luft Ballon, taken from the German language luft meaning air and ballon meaning ballon, in the sense that the luft balloon is a flying balloon. Luft Balloon is a flying Balloon Game that avoids the Birds that are coming to attack the Balloons. To survive an attack, all you have to do is dodge all the attacking Birds, in order to reach your goal.
